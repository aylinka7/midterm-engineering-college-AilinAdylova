Engineering College welcomes bright, motivated students from around the world. Our holistic admissions process evaluates academic excellence, leadership potential, and passion for engineering innovation.

International Students

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply to Engineering College

1

Complete Online Application

Fill out our comprehensive online application form with your personal information, academic history, and program preferences.

2

Submit Required Documents

Upload transcripts, test scores, letters of recommendation, and statement of purpose through our secure portal.

3

Application Review

Our admissions committee carefully evaluates your application holistically, considering all aspects of your profile.

4

Interview (if required)

Selected candidates may be invited for a virtual or in-person interview to discuss their goals and interests.

5

Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision within 4-6 weeks of completing your application and submitting all materials.

6

Enrollment Confirmation

Accept your offer, pay the enrollment deposit, and complete pre-arrival requirements to secure your place.

Admission Requirements

Domestic Students
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA: 3.0 on 4.0 scale
  • SAT (1200+) or ACT (25+) scores
  • Strong math and science background
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement (500-750 words)
International Students
  • All domestic requirements plus:
  • TOEFL (80+) or IELTS (6.5+)
  • Credential evaluation of transcripts
  • Proof of financial support
  • Copy of passport
  • Student visa eligibility
